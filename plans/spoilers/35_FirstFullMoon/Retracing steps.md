## Bar

They can feel that she's a vampire, plus she's an outsider that they don't know. "What is someone like you doing here?" One of them asks. 

"Just getting a drink." Sara orders a bloody mary. 

"We don't serve that here." The bartender crossed her arms and glared. 

"No blood fruit on hand? Well, then what about a salty llama?"
