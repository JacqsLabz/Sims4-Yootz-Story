Sara, Emma, and Brenda move to Strangerville to build a rocket. Don moves nearby. 

Sara & Don join the military career since that's a common job in the area & they want to blend in. They also spend some time at the local bar, since that's what many of their co-workers do. Mainly it's about keeping up appearances. 

Emma decides to pursue a career in science, since a job will help them blend in, and this could lead to her getting involved in something to help Brenda. But they're hiring **human** test subjects, so Emma takes the vampire cure as well so that she can get started. 

Brenda gets to move into a bedroom and maybe do online school? Maybe they agree in person school is too risky.
