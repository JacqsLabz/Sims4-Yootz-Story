![](../assets/img/02.04/06-16-26_10-25-52%20AM.png)

Sara knocked on the glass double doors. After a moment, Lilith came around the corner on the other side of the large windows in the doors. She opened the door, and stepped back to let Sara through, "Welcome fledgling."

"We need to talk." Sara said to Lilith, skipping any real greeting as she made her way in the door. 

"That is what we have a parlor for, this way please." Lilith lead Sara to the living room. "Caleb, this is Sara, the one I told you about." She said to the man sitting at the chess table, then turned to Sara. "Sara, this is my brother Caleb."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_6-50-14%20PM.png)

Caleb turned then stood to greet Sara. "Welcome to our home, fledgling." 

Sara didn't like the sound of 'fledgling' and her heart pounded; out of fear or anger, she wasn't sure which. She swallowed. The though *'he's like her'* echoed in her mind; Sara didn't know how she could tell, or even what the thought meant, just that knowing it made her even less at ease. "Charmed I'm sure." She turned to Lilith, "Can we talk, just you and me?"

"Yes, of course, always. Let's sit, shall we?" Lilith gestured to the couch and arm chairs on the other side of the room, then made her way over to them. 

Caleb gave her a slight smile, Sara could have sworn there was a glimmer from a fang in it, before he turned away from her. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-16-22%20PM.png)

As Caleb turned back to his chess table, Sara's stomach threatened to bring up her dinner right then and there. The nausea hit her so much faster than usual, and the pain was as sharp as ever. If she had had any idea what tonight would bring before she had ate, she probably would have skipped the meal. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-16-38%20PM.png)

But she couldn't change it now. So Sara swallowed, then closed her eyes for a moment as she tried to calm down by focusing on her breath. 

When she opened them, she turned to see Lilith in one of the armchairs. She took another steadying breath, and made her way over to her. 

"So what is this, some twisted vampire-act thing? I got back the memory of you pretending to bite my neck. What I don't know is what have you and your brother done to my gut. I've been pregnant before, I know what morning sickness feels like, but this? This is on a whole other level. What else did you do to me?" She hadn't even had the energy to be angry enough to need her workouts. Heck, she hadn't had half the energy she did at the moment in... she honestly didn't know how long, she'd been too tired to keep track. 

"Act?" Lilith whispered. Her hands went to her face as realization hit. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_6-54-32%20PM.png)

"Oh my," Lilith gasped. She paused. Her eyes went back and forth a few times. She swallowed. "Sara I thought you knew… you wanted it, I could sense it."

"Wanted what?" Sara just wanted to know what they did to her, and how to fix her rioting gut. 

Lilith let her fangs show. "The turning bite. My biting you was no fake or performance; my brother and I, we are both vampires. As you yourself now are." 

Sara couldn't… "How could I want something like that, vampires aren't..." She couldn't bring herself to say it. Sara swallowed, she felt as though she had been punched in the gut. Hard. 

"Something drew your soul to darkness, to the dark power and strength of vampires." Lilith stood, and stepped closer to Sara. 

Sara's head hurt, as her thoughts raced too fast to be words. Her hand went to her nauseous stomach. Her pregnant belly, as the bulge there reminded her. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_6-53-52%20PM.png)

Lilith continued, "Have you been able to connect with the darkness yet? Since you were drawn to it, it should come easy to you. Of course we can show you how to bend it to your will."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_6-53-20%20PM.png)

Sara's stomach churned along with her conflicting mixture of emotions. She swallowed a burp as one thought stood out above the racing haze of confusion her mind: she was about to lose the fight with her gut. "I'm going to be sick, do you even have a toilet?" she managed to get out. 

"Yes, back through the entry way." Lilith's eyebrow furrowed in concern. "On the other side of the stairs."


----






















----





On one hand, was a relief to understand what was happening with her body; and on the other, she didn't know how she felt about drinking blood. She had to admit: the idea of not painfully puking her guts out sounded wonderful. That alone made it sort tempting. But more importantly, the bottom line was that she was pregnant. Regardless of how she felt about it, there was no getting around how much her body needed proper nutrition. So Sara decided she was willing to try it once, to see how it felt.

"I have so many questions for you." Sara swallowed, her throat was suddenly so dry, "You don't mind if I come back some other time ask them, right?" Sara held out her hands with a nervous smile.

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-06-53%20PM.png)


"Of course not." Lilith smiled wide, "I turned you, I'd be happy to help you." 

"Then I'll stick to one question for the moment. How do I make sure I don't turn anyone by mistake?" But even as she asked it, another memory entered her mind's eye, of her biting Lilith's arm before. Somehow she knew it was key. 

"Oh that is easy. Turning another requires they drink your blood after you drink theirs. Pretty easy to avoid doing so by mistake." 

Sara nodded. "Then how do I... go about it?" She didn't even know where to start. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-07-18%20PM.png)

"Biting another requires using the dark power within you." Lilith stood, "Come this way, my brother is better at teaching that than myself."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-09-40%20PM.png)

"You have to learn to sense the dark power both in and around you." They sat as Caleb explained the principles behind using the dark vampire power. 

Before long, it was time for a little demonstration as Caleb showed her how to put theory into practice. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-13-56%20PM.png)

"Once you can feel the dark power, gather it."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-14-07%20PM.png)

"Then expel it to do your bidding!" After his feet returned to the floor, "First try sensing for the dark power."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-15-34%20PM.png)

Sara let instinct guide her, and found that she could feel and pull in the dark magic of the vampires rather easily.

"You're quite the natural." Caleb remarked. "I've never seen a newly turned fledgling vampire take such command of it so quickly."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-08-51%20PM.png)

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-08-23%20PM.png)

Sara left the Vatore siblings ready to put what she had learned to use.

----

So when she found some strangers, she let instinct take over and the darkness guide her.

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-19-22%20PM.png)

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-18-25%20PM.png)

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-18-40%20PM.png)

When her fangs broke the flesh, the flavor that filled her mouth was the best thing she had ever tasted. When she took her first swallow, her body seemed to brace, nothing else she had swallowed recently had felt good. But then the blood hit what was left of her stomach, and Sara found it had to stop even after several swallows. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-20-53%20PM.png)

But this was exactly why she had made sure there were two people nearby: 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-21-06%20PM.png)

In case one wasn't enough. 

After two back-to-back deep feedings, Sara breathed as she held her belly and took in the sensations running through her body. It wasn't like being full from food when she had been human. This was something else entirely. Her thirst was so completely satisfied and every part of her hummed with energy. 

Sara felt so energized that she jogged the whole way home, and then still felt like she had energy to spare. She found it hard to sit still as she waited for Jane to wake up. 

----

When the family was awake, Sara and Jane went outside so they weren't overheard by the girls. 

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-40-04%20PM.png)

"So I have some odd news, well, good news, I know why I've felt so sick lately... I got bit by a vampire."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-40-11%20PM.png)

"A vampire?" At first, Jane thought she must be joking.

"I know it sounds crazy, but they turned me. Apparently being pregnant during the change can make things extra... tricky." 

"You're serious... are you going be ok? Is the baby...?"

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-40-22%20PM.png)

Sara put her hands to her belly, "We're both fine, just vampires now." It was another one of those things where Sara could feel it: the child within her had that same dark magic that she had felt in Lilith and Caleb. 

Jane's face dropped even more, so Sara put her arms around Jane and pulled her close.

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-41-26%20PM.png)

"I promise, we're both ok, me and the baby. You can feel for yourself."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-42-33%20PM.png)

"Ok, yeah."

![](../assets/img/02.04/12-12-17_7-40-47%20PM.png)

"Wow, that kick feels stronger than Emma and Savanna." Jane smiled.

"Tell me about it." Sara held her belly and tried not to wince at the pain.
