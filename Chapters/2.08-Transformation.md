
*The next morning...*

"Hey sweetie, how you doing?" Sara asked.

"I don't think I got any sleep last night." Jane struggled to keep her eyes open.

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-19-17_11-08-11%20PM.png)

"How about I make you some breakfast for once?" Sara smiled. "See how your stomach takes it?"

Jane nodded.

When it was finished, Sara sat down next to her again.

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-20-17_8-12-21%20AM.png)

"One omelet, with love." Sara blew a kiss. 

"You're too cute sweetie." Jane smiled.

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-20-17_8-12-10%20AM.png)

"Let me know what you think; at this point I don't think my taste for food will ever come back." 

Jane took a tentative bite. After the chewed and swallowed, "It tastes fine, thank you sweetie."

Sara smiled. "You're welcome. I know this a bit last minute, but Don invited us to a party later today, did you want to come with me?"

After she finished another bite, "Yeah, that sounds nice. A little coffee should help wake me up."

"Cool. I think he said it's a birthday party for one of his friends?"

"Oh..." Jane held her stomach as pain filled her middle.

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-20-17_8-23-02%20AM.png)

"Ow." Jane sucked in air through her teeth, making a slight hiss, "Does this mean I won't be able to eat either?"

"Well, for me there was this really nauseous feeling, and the flavor was beyond gross. Some pain is normal, but getting sick like I did isn't."

Jane waited for the feeling to pass, then went back to enjoying the meal as they chatted. 

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-20-17_8-24-23%20AM.png)

Jane finished the first then asked for seconds.

Sara raised an eyebrow, "Really? Are you sure you want that?"

"You're immoral, right? Who wouldn't want that?"

Sara nodded, back up a little, and channeled the dark energy of the vampires.

![](../assets/img/02.08/12-20-17_6-00-18%20PM.png)
