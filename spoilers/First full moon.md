Cass goes into the safe room for the full moon. They have some meat (and maybe any other supplies?) in there. Sara and Don take turns fighting the wolf. The wolf is extra/especially violent, more than before. 

While Don is fighting it, Sara gets time to just sit and observe. To feel with that dark power of the vampires. And what she can feel is that this new magic is (somehow) angry. She doesn't understand how, but it reminds her of her own anger.
