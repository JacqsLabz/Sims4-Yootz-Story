Sara and Don are alone after Cass is in control again.

Don crossed his arms. "How you holding up? You ok Sara?" 

Sara looked up and sighed. "Actually, I'm fine." She leaned on the wall, facing him. "I mean I know she's not, at least she feels like she's not. And I know Jane is scared. And I know all of this *looks* **bad**. I mean really bad. But I'm actually ok."

"I just want us on the same page here. So are you sure?" 

Sara held out her hands. "I can feel it. The magic. Cass is still in there, even when she's a wolf. And the power that's changing her, it's... it's not bad." She paused, "I don't really got words for it, but I know she's gonna be ok. I don't really know how, but I know we can handle this."
